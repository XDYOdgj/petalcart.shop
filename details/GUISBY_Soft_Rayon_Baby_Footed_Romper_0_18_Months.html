Soft and Breathable Baby Footed Pajamas for Newborns

Crafted with a luxurious blend of 95% rayon derived from bamboo and 5% spandex, these GUISBY baby footed pajamas deliver an unmatched level of comfort that feels like a second skin. The soft, skin-friendly fabric is gentle on your baby's sensitive skin, making it ideal for everyday wear or special occasions like baby showers and first birthdays.

Designed with fold-over mittens, these pajamas help keep your baby's hands warm during cooler nights while also preventing any unwanted scratching. The breathable and moisture-wicking properties ensure your little one stays comfortable and dry throughout the day, whether they're sleeping or playing.

These pajamas are tailored for newborns and babies up to 18 months, designed to grow with your little one and maintain a perfect fit as they develop.
